About Warner Bros.

Warner Bros. is a global leader in entertainment, delighting audiences for over a century. Founded in 1923 by four brothers with a passion for storytelling, we've grown into a powerhouse bringing movies, television, animation, and video games to life. From iconic characters like Bugs Bunny and Harry Potter to groundbreaking franchises like DC Comics and The Lord of the Rings, we're dedicated to pushing the boundaries of entertainment and creating unforgettable experiences for fans around the world
